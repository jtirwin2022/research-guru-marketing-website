Why This App?

College students need a more intuitive, accessible way to find and evaluate sources for their papers because library search engines are foreign to them and are hard to use.

Learning by Doing

"Developing a good research question is important. Students must practice to develop this kind of thinking process." -- Nadine, college instructor

Sorting with Labels

"My teacher said I need to find more scholarly sources. I thought the websites I used for my rough draft were credible. I'm confused." -- Angel, 1st year college

Finding Keywords

Improving Access

The Impact

Stakeholders from different walks of life are coming to the table to develop this app. Research Guru doesn't replace the invaluable services that teachers and librarians provide but instead supports students on their journey through college. Students have more time for writing and thinking.
